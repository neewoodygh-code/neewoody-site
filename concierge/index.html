What is Carpentry Concierge?

A members' network for Ghanaian carpenters, vendors and couriers — built by Neewoody. Clients get a trusted artisan without the guesswork; artisans get visibility, work, sourcing and safety tools in one place.

Vetted directory
Real, verified artisans by trade and area — hire members, member-to-member.

A jobs board, a “buy for me” errand board, and vendor storefronts.
Tools & safety
Job pricing, cut lists, and a lone-worker safety check-in.
