The Workshop

A table is the centre of the room. We build it that way.

Every dining table we make starts with a conversation about the space — how many seats, what timber, what finish, how it will be used daily and how it should feel when guests arrive. We draw it, cut it, finish it and install it ourselves.

We work in Ghanaian hardwoods like Afromosia, Hyedua and Guarea, and in pine with welded steel frames for a more industrial feel. For restaurants and hospitality venues, we build at volume — matching sets delivered as a managed project from brief to handover.

The pages that follow show real tables in clients' dining rooms, kitchens and restaurants. No renders, no stock photography — just the work.
