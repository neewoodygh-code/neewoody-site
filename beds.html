What We Build

Types of Custom Beds

From minimalist frames to fully upholstered statement pieces — browse our most popular styles.

Platform Beds

Low-profile, modern frames with solid or slatted bases. No box spring needed. Great for contemporary bedroom aesthetics and good airflow under the mattress.

Storage Beds

Maximise every centimetre. We build beds with side-opening drawers or a hydraulic lift-up base — ideal for smaller bedrooms where wardrobe space is limited.

Bunk Beds & Triple Bunks

Designed and built for safety as much as space-saving. Solid construction, fitted ladders, and guard rails — sized exactly for the room and the children using them.

Upholstered Headboard Beds

A wooden frame combined with a padded, fabric-covered headboard for a hotel-bedroom feel. Choose your own fabric colour and texture to match your décor.

Poster & Statement Beds

For the bedroom that deserves a centrepiece. We craft four-poster and column-post designs that command attention — in traditional or contemporary interpretations.

Cabin & Loft Beds

Elevated sleeping platform with usable space below — a desk, sofa, or wardrobe. A popular choice for children's rooms and studio apartments in Accra.

What Affects the Price

Custom Bed Pricing in Accra

Every bed is priced individually based on your brief. Here's what influences the final figure:

📏
Size

Single and double frames cost less than king or super-king. Non-standard sizes are quoted per project.

🪵
Material

Hardwood frames are priced higher than MDF or softwood. Veneer and painted finishes vary by complexity.

🗄️
Storage Features

Under-bed drawers or a hydraulic lift-up base add to the cost but are often worth it for smaller rooms.

🎨
Headboard Style

Simple wooden headboards are the most affordable. Upholstered designs with fabric or leather cost more.

✨
Finish & Detail

Carved details, inlays, or multi-tone finishes add time and materials to the project.

📍
Delivery Location

Delivery and assembly within Accra is standard. Locations outside Accra may carry an additional charge.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a custom bed?

Most bed frames take 2–4 weeks from order confirmation to delivery. More complex builds with storage, upholstery, or intricate detailing may take up to 6 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline when you confirm your order.

Can you match an existing bedroom set?

Yes. If you have existing bedroom furniture, share photos and dimensions with us. We'll match the stain colour, wood grain, and finish as closely as possible so the new piece blends in seamlessly.

Do you build beds for children?

Absolutely. We build bunk beds, cabin beds, and loft beds with children's safety as a top priority — solid joints, fitted guard rails, and smooth edges throughout. We can also build fun themed designs for younger children.

What mattress sizes do you build for?

We build for any mattress size — standard single (90×190cm), double (135×190cm), queen (150×200cm), king (160×200cm), or completely custom dimensions. Just tell us your mattress size and we build around it.

Can I add a headboard later?

It's easier (and often cheaper) to include the headboard in the original build. Retrofitting a headboard is possible but requires re-working the frame. We'd recommend deciding upfront — show us inspiration photos and we'll quote both options.

Do you deliver and assemble in Accra?

Yes — delivery and assembly is included for all locations within Accra. We bring the pieces, assemble the bed in your room, and remove any packaging. For areas outside Accra, contact us for a delivery quote.
