The Process

How It Works

From first message to finished installation — here's what to expect when you work with Neewoody.

01

Get in Touch

Send us a WhatsApp message or fill in our quote form. Tell us what you have in mind — rough ideas are fine at this stage.

02

Site Visit & Quote

We visit your space, take measurements, and discuss materials and finishes. You'll receive a clear, itemised quote within a few days.

03

We Build It

Once the design is agreed and a deposit is paid, we build your furniture. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

04

Installation

We deliver and install your furniture, make sure everything is level and secure, and tidy up before we leave.

Custom Furniture in Accra

Neewoody Custom Woodwork specialises in made-to-measure furniture designed and built for homes and properties across Accra, Ghana. Whether you're looking for custom wardrobes in Accra, a new kitchen fit-out, or a bespoke bed frame, every piece we make is designed specifically for your space — not for a showroom floor. We don't sell off-the-shelf; we measure your room, design around it, and build to fit.

Our carpentry services in Accra cover the full range of home furniture: built-in wardrobes that use every centimetre of wall space, custom beds with integrated storage, kitchen cabinets designed for your exact layout, shelving, TV units, and more. We work in all kinds of finishes — natural hardwood, painted MDF, high-gloss lacquer, and textured laminate — so the final result matches your existing décor or creates a look that's entirely new. Every build is handled in-house, from design through to installation.

If you're a homeowner, developer, or interior designer in Accra looking for reliable, high-quality bespoke furniture — the kind that looks like it was always there — Neewoody is built for that brief. We serve clients in East Legon, Cantonments, Airport Residential, Spintex, Tema, Adenta, Labone, and throughout Greater Accra. Get in touch via WhatsApp and we'll arrange a site visit at no charge.
