The Process

How It Works

From first message to finished installation — here's what to expect when you work with Neewoody.

01

Get in Touch

Send us a WhatsApp message or fill in our quote form. Tell us what you have in mind — rough ideas are fine at this stage.

02

Site Visit & Quote

We visit your space, take measurements, and discuss materials and finishes. You'll receive a clear, itemised quote within a few days.

03

We Build It

Once the design is agreed and a deposit is paid, we build your furniture. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

04

Installation

We deliver and install your furniture, make sure everything is level and secure, and tidy up before we leave.
