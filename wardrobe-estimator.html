How are estimates calculated? This tool uses Neewoody's actual cost model — workshop overhead, labour rates, materials, hardware, and logistics — scaled by wardrobe size, door type, interior layout, and finish. The range shown (±12%) reflects real variation in material costs and job complexity. The midpoint aligns with our Standard Build tier. Premium finishes, complex interiors, or challenging site access may push toward or above the upper end.
