Why Custom?

A Wardrobe Built for Your Room, Not Borrowed from a Showroom

Most homes in Accra don't have standard dimensions. Ceilings are higher or lower than expected, walls aren't perfectly square, and alcoves create awkward spaces that off-the-shelf wardrobes never quite fill.

A Neewoody custom wardrobe is designed around your actual room — measured, drawn up, built, and installed to fit perfectly from wall to wall and floor to ceiling. No filler panels, no gaps, no half-measures.

We build wardrobes for bedrooms across Accra — from single-room apartments to master bedrooms in larger homes. Every project gets the same level of care and attention to detail.

What We Build

Types of Wardrobes We Make

Every style is available in the size, finish, and internal layout that works for your bedroom.

Sliding Door Wardrobes

Space-saving sliding doors in wood, mirror, or glass. Ideal for rooms where swing clearance is limited. Two or three-panel configurations available.

Hinged Door Wardrobes

Classic full-access hinged doors for a traditional or contemporary look. Available in single, double, or multi-door layouts with soft-close hinges.

Floor-to-Ceiling Wardrobes

Maximum height storage that reaches the ceiling — great for high-ceilinged rooms common in Accra homes. Can include a top section for less-used items.

Walk-in Wardrobes

If you have the space, a walk-in wardrobe changes how you start your morning. We design open dressing areas with shelves, rails, drawers, and optional island units.

Alcove & Fitted Storage

We make use of awkward spaces — alcoves, sloped ceilings, and narrow recesses — turning them into useful, built-in storage.

Custom Interior Layouts

Rails, shelves, drawers, shoe racks, tie holders, and accessories — we configure the inside of your wardrobe to suit how you actually organise your things.

Materials & Finish

What We Build With

We work with a range of materials to suit different budgets and aesthetics. We'll recommend what's right for your project during the consultation.

Melamine-Faced Boards

Durable, moisture-resistant, and available in a wide range of colours and wood-effect finishes. The most common and cost-effective choice.

MDF with Paint Finish

Smooth, paintable finish for a seamless, modern look. Ideal when the wardrobe needs to match wall colour or achieve a built-in, joinery feel.

Solid Wood Accents

Natural hardwood can be incorporated for shelving, drawer fronts, or handles to add warmth and a premium feel to your wardrobe.

Mirror Doors

Mirror-fronted sliding panels create the illusion of more space and add practicality. Available in full-length or framed styles.

Pricing

What Affects the Cost of a Custom Wardrobe?

We don't publish fixed prices because every wardrobe is different. These are the main factors that influence the final cost.

Size & Dimensions

Larger wardrobes use more material and take more time to build and install.

Door Style

Sliding doors cost more than hinged due to the track mechanism. Mirror or glass doors add to this further.

Material Choice

Melamine boards are the most economical. Solid wood, high-gloss, or lacquered finishes cost more.

Internal Fittings

Drawers, shoe racks, tie holders, and accessories add functionality — and some cost — to the interior.

Complexity

A straight run is simpler than a design with angles, integrated lighting, or unusual dimensions.

Location

We serve Accra and Greater Accra. Longer distances may include a small transport charge.
