Jerksoul Restaurant

A full commercial rooftop dining fit-out — custom timber pergola, 15+ branded dining sets, and a reclaimed-style bar counter.

Client Jerksoul Restaurant
Year 2022
Location Accra, Ghana
Category Commercial Hospitality
Scope Full rooftop dining fit-out

The Brief

A Rooftop Restaurant Built to Impress

Jerksoul Restaurant came to Neewoody with a clear brief: transform a bare rooftop into a distinctive commercial dining destination. The space needed to work hard — functional enough for a busy restaurant, durable enough for daily commercial use, and distinctive enough to become part of the Jerksoul brand experience.

The commission covered the full fit-out from overhead structure to seating: a custom timber pergola with polycarbonate roofing, a complete set of branded dining furniture, and a centrepiece bar counter. Every element had to work together cohesively — and hold up under the demands of an active hospitality environment.

What Was Built

The Full Scope

Every element of the rooftop was designed and built by Neewoody — from the overhead timber structure down to the branded glass table tops.

⛺
Custom Timber Pergola Full rooftop pergola structure in pine with polycarbonate roofing panels — designed to provide shade and weather protection while keeping the space bright and open.
🍽
15+ Branded Dining Sets Pine dining tables with custom Issacher branded glass tops, paired with upholstered cube stools — built for commercial durability with a distinctive visual identity.
🍹
Bar Counter Large interior bar counter with reclaimed-style timber panel cladding and a solid wood top — the centrepiece of the rooftop, built to handle the pace of a busy service environment.
🪑
Upholstered Cube Stools Custom upholstered stools paired to the dining sets and bar counter throughout the rooftop — padded for comfort and built for the wear of commercial use.
