Full Dining Room Commission in Hyedua

Dining table, six upholstered chairs, and a console unit — three pieces, one timber, one brief.

Client Judith, East Legon
Year 2022
Location East Legon, Accra
Species Hyedua (Tarrietia utilis)
Scope Full dining room commission

The Commission

One Room. One Timber. Three Pieces.

Judith came to Neewoody with a clear brief for her East Legon dining room: solid hardwood throughout, a unified look across every piece, and a rich dark finish that would anchor the space. The commission covered three furniture items designed to work together as a set — a large-format dining table, six matching upholstered chairs, and a console unit.

The timber selected was Hyedua — a dense West African hardwood with a naturally deep, warm tone that takes a dark espresso finish exceptionally well. All three pieces were built in the Neewoody workshop, finished in the same species and stain, and delivered and installed in the client's East Legon home.

About the Timber

Hyedua — Tarrietia utilis

Hyedua is a dense, heavy West African hardwood with a naturally deep, warm tone and a tight, even grain. It is harvested across Ghana and Côte d'Ivoire and has long been used in high-quality furniture and joinery for its structural stability and its ability to take a dark stain evenly and richly.

Finished in an espresso tone, Hyedua produces furniture with a depth of colour that reads as genuinely premium — without relying on veneers, laminates, or surface treatments to achieve the look. The pieces in this commission were designed specifically to let the timber do the work.
