Why Solid Wood?

Built to Last. Built to Be Passed On.

Most furniture in Accra is particle board with a laminate surface — functional, affordable, but with a lifespan measured in years rather than decades. Solid wood is different. A well-built solid wood table or bed frame doesn't just survive daily use — it develops character over time, can be sanded and refinished, and holds its structural integrity for generations.

We work with locally sourced and imported timber depending on the piece and the client's brief — sapele, teak, mahogany, iroko, and others available. Every piece is jointed, not just screwed, and finished for the humidity and temperature of the Accra climate.

Good to Know

Solid Wood Takes Longer. That's Part of the Point.

Solid wood jobs take longer to produce than board-based furniture — timber needs to be selected, dried properly, jointed, assembled, and finished in stages. Lead times for solid wood pieces are typically 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. If you're working to a deadline, tell us early and we'll plan accordingly.
