A TV Unit Designed for Your Room — Not Picked Off a Shelf

Most Accra living rooms have one thing in common — the TV is fighting the room. Wrong height, wrong width, cables everywhere, no storage that actually works. A shelf from the market or a flat-pack unit never quite fits the wall, the screen size, or the way you actually use the space.

What We Build

Types of TV Units We Make

Floating Wall Units

Wall-mounted with no legs touching the floor — clean, modern, and makes the room feel larger. We build in cable routing so nothing hangs loose.

Floor-to-Ceiling Feature Walls

The TV becomes part of a full wall composition — shelving above, storage below, display niches beside it. A complete feature wall rather than a standalone unit.

Media Consoles & Sideboards

A lower console unit with drawers, open bays for equipment, and a clean top surface. Works in living rooms and bedrooms alike.

Alcove & Recessed Builds

If your room has an alcove or recessed wall, we can build a unit that fills it perfectly — no gaps, no visible side panels, fully integrated.

What Goes Into It

Built to Handle Everything in Your Living Room

Cable Management Built In

Routed from screen to decoder to wall socket — hidden inside the unit from the start, not taped on afterward.

Right Height for Your Screen

We calculate the ideal viewing height based on your seating and screen size — no neck strain, no awkward tilt.

Storage That Actually Works

Dedicated bays for your decoder, streaming box, game console, router — sized exactly so everything fits and ventilates properly.

Ventilation for Equipment

Decoders and amplifiers run hot. We design open bays or vented backs so your equipment doesn't overheat and fail early.

Finish to Match Your Interior

PVC wrap, veneer, paint finish, or a mix — we match the unit to your existing furniture and colour scheme.

Any Budget, Any Size

A simple floating console or a full feature wall — we scope the job to fit what you want to spend without cutting corners on quality.
