Common questions about custom TV units.

How long does it take to build a custom TV unit?
Most TV units take 2–4 weeks from order confirmation to installation. Larger feature wall builds may take up to 6 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline when we confirm your order — we'd rather under-promise and deliver early than the reverse.
Do you handle cable management as part of the build?
Yes — cable routing is planned during the design phase, not added as an afterthought. We route cables through the structure of the unit so they're completely hidden. If your wall needs conduit run before the unit is installed, we can advise on that too.
Can I choose the finish and colour?
Completely. We work in PVC wrap laminates (hundreds of finishes available — wood grain, solid colour, matte, gloss), painted MDF, or veneer. If you want to match an existing wardrobe or kitchen, bring a photo and we'll find the closest match.
What if I want to add open shelving for books or display items?
That's a natural part of the design conversation. We commonly build units that combine a closed lower section for equipment with open shelving above for books, plants, or ornaments. Tell us what you want to display and we design around it.
Do you install as well as build?
Yes — installation is included for all locations within Accra. We bring the unit, mount or position it, run the cables, and leave the room tidy. For locations outside Accra, contact us for a quote that includes travel.
Can I get a quote before committing?
Always. Send us your wall dimensions, what equipment you need to house, and a rough idea of the style you're after. We'll come back with a clear price. No obligation, and site visits are free within Accra.
