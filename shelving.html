Why Built-In?

Shelving Built for Your Walls and Your Life

A freestanding bookshelf never quite reaches the ceiling. An alcove always has that awkward gap. Floating shelves from a hardware store come in fixed widths that don't match your wall. Built-in shelving solves all of that — wall to wall, floor to ceiling if you want it, every centimetre used.

We build shelving for home offices, living rooms, studies, children's rooms, and corridors — anywhere you need organised, accessible storage that looks like it was always meant to be there.

Types of Shelving We Make

Alcove Shelving

Chimney breast alcoves, recessed walls, and awkward nooks — we fill them exactly. Open shelves above, cupboards below, or a full open bookcase.

Home Office Shelving

Integrated shelving around a desk, above a workstation, or across a full wall — filing, books, and equipment all in reach and organised.

Display & Feature Shelving

Open floating shelves for décor, books, and plants — spaced correctly for what you're displaying, not just whatever the standard pitch is.

Utility & Laundry Storage

Shelving for store rooms, pantries, laundry areas, and garages — built to handle the weight of what you're storing, not just look good.
