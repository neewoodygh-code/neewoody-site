Common questions about custom shelving.

How long does it take to build custom shelving?
Most shelving jobs take 1–3 weeks from confirmation to installation. Full-wall or multi-room commissions may take 3–5 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline when we confirm your brief.
Can you match my existing shelving or furniture finish?
Yes. Bring photos and dimensions. We'll match the colour, finish, and hardware as closely as possible so the new piece integrates with what you already have.
Do you build shelving with integrated lighting?
Yes — we can route LED strip lighting into recessed channels along the underside of shelves. Specify this when you enquire and we'll include it in the design and quote.
What materials do you use?
Primarily moisture-resistant MDF or moisture-resistant board with PVC or paint finishes for most interiors. For pantries and laundry areas we specify boards rated for the environment. We can also work in solid wood if the brief calls for it.
Do you handle wall fixings and installation?
Yes — delivery and installation is included for all jobs in Accra. We fix directly to the wall, make good around the edges, and remove all packaging and offcuts.
