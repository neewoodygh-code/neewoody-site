Hyedua Dining Table — East Legon

Built in genuine West African hardwood.

We work in timber that grows here — species selected for their durability, grain character, and how they age in the Accra climate. The wood is part of the brief.

Afromosia

Pericopsis elata

A premium West African hardwood with natural oil content, fine grain, and a warm honey tone. Highly durable, stable in humid climates, and an exceptional finish timber. Often specified for statement dining tables, live-edge pieces, and outdoor-facing installations.

Hyedua

Tarrietia utilis

A dense, heavy hardwood with a naturally deep, warm tone and tight even grain. Takes a dark stain evenly and richly — ideal for dining sets, console pieces, and full room commissions where a unified, premium finish across multiple pieces is the brief.
