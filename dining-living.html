Hardwood Species

Built in Genuine West African Hardwood

We work in timber that grows here — species selected for their durability, grain, and how they age in the Accra climate.

Afromosia — Pericopsis elata

A premium West African hardwood with natural oil content, fine grain, and a warm honey tone. Highly durable, stable in humid climates, and an exceptional finish timber. Often used for statement dining tables and live-edge pieces.

Hyedua — Tarrietia utilis

A dense, heavy hardwood with a naturally deep, warm tone and tight even grain. Takes a dark stain evenly and richly — ideal for dining sets, console pieces, and full room commissions where a unified, premium finish is the brief.
