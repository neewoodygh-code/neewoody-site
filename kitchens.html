Common questions about custom kitchens.

How long does a kitchen fit-out take?
A typical kitchen fit-out takes 4–8 weeks from order confirmation to installation — design finalisation, build time, and installation. Smaller jobs (a few replacement units) can be faster. We'll confirm your timeline at the quote stage.
Do you supply the countertop too?
We supply and fit timber and MDF worktops. For stone, granite, or quartz countertops, we can coordinate with your supplier so everything aligns correctly — or recommend a specialist if needed.
Can you work around my existing appliances?
Yes. We measure your appliances and build precise housings around them. If you're getting new appliances, share the model numbers and dimensions early so we can plan the layout accordingly.
Can I just replace a few cabinets rather than the whole kitchen?
Absolutely. We can replace individual broken or dated units and match them as closely as possible to your existing kitchen. It's more cost-effective than a full replacement if the rest is still in good shape.
Do your kitchens hold up in Ghana's climate?
We design for it. We use moisture-resistant board for base cabinets, quality edge banding, and finishes that stand up to heat and humidity. We also advise on ventilation and cabinet placement during the design stage.
How do I get started?
Send us a WhatsApp message with photos of your kitchen and a rough idea of what you'd like. We'll arrange a free site visit, take measurements, and send you a detailed quote within a few days.
