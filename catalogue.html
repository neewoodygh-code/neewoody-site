The Workshop

Furniture made to measure, not made to fit.

Neewoody Custom Woodwork is a bespoke furniture and carpentry workshop based on Amugi Avenue in Accra. Every commission begins with the room it is built for — its dimensions, its light, the way it will be used — and is drawn, cut, finished and installed by our own crew.

We work in laminate and solid hardwood alike: fitted wardrobes and kitchens, dining sets and beds, live-edge tables, and full institutional fitouts. The pages that follow show real installations in clients' homes and premises — no renders, no stock photography.
